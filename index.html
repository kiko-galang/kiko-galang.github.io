About

I'm a fourth-year PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Adam Weber and Alex Bell at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. My research develops concentrated solution theory frameworks for ion transport in ion-exchange membranes, sitting at the intersection of transport phenomena, molecular thermodynamics, and polymer physics.

Research

My thesis focuses on generalizing concentrated solution theory to anion exchange membranes, with applications to electrolyzers and fuel cells.
